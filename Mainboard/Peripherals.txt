BK3254 
Pros: FM, BT 4.1 and SD all-in-one SoC supporting Stereo audio and voice output, plenty of GPIOs
Cons: Datasheet is confidential, closed-source and cannot be modified past certain level

ESP32
Pros: Powerful SoC with Bluetooth and 802.11n Wi-Fi built in
Cons: Every hardware and software interactions need to be programmed
